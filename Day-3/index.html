CPC Overview

The Central Placement Cell is to empower students to achieve their career aspirations by providing them with the necessary support, guidance, and resources. The CPC is committed to promoting a culture of lifelong learning and to helping students acquire the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in a rapidly changing job market.
